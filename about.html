About S3 Capital

Building the future of middle-market real estate lending through expertise, discipline, and partnership.

Institutional Capital Meets Middle-Market Expertise

S3 Capital was founded in 2013 to address a significant gap in the market: the lack of institutional-quality construction and bridge financing for middle-market real estate developers. Since inception, we have established ourselves as one of the most active private lenders in the New York metropolitan region, with expanding national presence across key markets.

Led by Principals Joshua Crane and Robert Schwartz, our platform combines deep market relationships with rigorous credit discipline. We originate and manage a diversified portfolio of construction and bridge loans ranging from $5 million to $250 million, with particular expertise in the middle-market segment where institutional capital and specialized knowledge create significant value.

Our vertically integrated approach sets us apart. With dedicated teams across origination, underwriting, credit, construction management, asset management, and finance, we deliver certainty of execution to developers while maintaining disciplined risk management that generates consistent, risk-adjusted returns for our investors.

Whether providing flexible capital solutions to experienced developers or delivering institutional-quality investment opportunities to sophisticated capital partners, S3 Capital operates at the intersection of middle-market opportunity and institutional discipline‚Äîa position that has enabled us to originate over $8.2 billion across 820+ transactions since inception.

The S3 Capital Advantage

Developer-Led Perspective

Our principals' background as real estate developers provides unique insight into the challenges borrowers face. This perspective enables us to structure creative, practical solutions that address real-world project needs while maintaining appropriate risk parameters.

Vertically Integrated Platform

From origination through construction oversight to final repayment, our in-house team manages every aspect of the lending process. This integration ensures consistent execution, proactive problem-solving, and superior outcomes for all stakeholders.

Middle-Market Focus

We specialize in the $5M to $250M segment where institutional capital meets middle-market opportunity. This focus allows us to deliver the sophistication and certainty typically reserved for larger transactions while maintaining the flexibility and responsiveness essential for middle-market deals.

Proven Track Record

With over $8.2 billion originated across 820+ transactions since 2013, we have demonstrated consistent performance through multiple market cycles. Our track record reflects both our underwriting discipline and our ability to navigate challenging market conditions successfully.

Comprehensive In-House Expertise

Led by Principals Joshua Crane and Robert Schwartz, our integrated team of industry professionals ensures the success of every transaction from origination through final payment.
