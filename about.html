About S3 Capital

A decade of excellence in institutional-quality middle-market construction and bridge lending.

Delivering Risk-Adjusted Returns Through Market Expertise

S3 Capital was founded in 2013 to address a significant gap in institutional-quality, middle-market construction lending. Since inception, we have established ourselves as one of the most active private lenders in the New York metropolitan region with expanding national presence across key markets.

Under the leadership of Principals Joshua Crane and Robert Schwartz, our platform combines deep market relationships with rigorous credit discipline. We originate and manage a diversified portfolio of construction and bridge loans ranging from $5 million to $250 million, with particular expertise in the middle-market segment where institutional capital and specialized knowledge create significant value.
